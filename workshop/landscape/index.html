The number of available Patterns has significantly increased with more involvement from the field and our partners. Take a look at the following graphic - at the beginning of 2024, we had a few Patterns delivered by our Partners and few more from the Patterns team and field.

As of June 2026, we can see how far the project has come!

Patterns Library Links

The easiest and fastest way to find out what Validated Patterns are available is to check out the Patterns tab on this site.

Each pattern will have a link to its source code but you can also browse our Github org directly. Additional patterns may be available in the sandbox org.

Validated Patterns are an evolution of how you deploy applications in a hybrid cloud. With a pattern, you can automatically deploy a full application stack through a GitOps-based framework. With this framework, you can create business-centric solutions while maintaining a level of Continuous Integration (CI) over your application.
