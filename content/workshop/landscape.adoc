== Patterns Library Links

The easiest and fastest way to find out what Validated Patterns are available is to check out the link:/patterns[Patterns tab on this site.]

[#github]

Each pattern will have a link to its source code but you can also browse our link:https://github.com/orgs/validatedpatterns/repositories[Github org] directly.
Additional patterns may be available in the link:https://github.com/orgs/validatedpatterns-sandbox/repositories[sandbox org].
